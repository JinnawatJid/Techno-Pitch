# ShopDer Pitch Deck Roadmap

This roadmap tracks our progress towards the 7-day pitch deadline.

## Phase 1: Foundation (Current Focus)
- [ ] **Problem Definition**
    - [ ] Research "Friction" in current second-hand platforms (FB Marketplace, Kaidee, Instagram)
    - [ ] Validate the "Boredom/Discovery" hypothesis (Why people buy new vs. used)
    - [ ] Connect "Green Living Lab" mission to user pain points (Waste vs. Convenience)
- [ ] **Market Validation**
    - [ ] Sizing the Second-Hand Fashion Market in Thailand/SEA
    - [ ] Identifying Gen Z/Millennial sustainability trends in Thailand
    - [ ] "Circular Economy" adoption rates

## Phase 2: Solution & Mechanics (Next)
- [ ] **Concept Validation**
    - [ ] Psychology of "Swipe" mechanics in commerce (Tinder for X)
    - [ ] Case studies of successful "Discovery Commerce" apps (Depop, Poshmark, TikTok Shop)
- [ ] **Unique Value Proposition (UVP)**
    - [ ] Define what makes ShopDer 10x better than competitors
    - [ ] Gamification elements (rewards for green behavior?)

## Phase 3: Business & Strategy
- [ ] **Customer Segment**
    - [ ] Define the specific "Beachhead" persona (e.g., "Eco-conscious Gen Z Student")
- [ ] **Revenue Stream**
    - [ ] Explore potential models: Commission, Ads, Premium features
- [ ] **Channels**
    - [ ] Go-to-market strategy (University ambassadors? TikTok influencers?)
