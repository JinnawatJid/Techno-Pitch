# Research Findings: Aligned with Pitch Deck Structure

This document organizes our research data to directly support the 10-slide ShopDer Pitch Deck.

## Slide 1: Title & Vision
**"ShopDer: Tinder for Sustainable Fashion"**
*   **Core Concept:** The "Green Living Lab." We are not just a marketplace; we are a behavioral experiment to make sustainability addictive.
*   **Mission:** Shift consumption from *New* to *Pre-loved* by removing friction and adding fun.

## Slide 2: The Problem (The Why)
**"Fashion is Destroying the Planet, and Saving it is Hard."**
*   **Environmental Crisis:**
    *   Fashion is the #2 global polluter, responsible for **10% of carbon emissions** [4].
    *   **92 million tonnes** of textile waste are dumped annually [5].
    *   Garment usage has dropped by **36% in 15 years** [5].
*   **User Friction (The Gap):**
    *   **Trust:** "Will I get scammed?" is the #1 fear on FB Marketplace [2].
    *   **Effort:** Searching for items on Kaidee is like "looking for a needle in a haystack."
    *   **Boredom:** Traditional platforms are functional, not inspirational. Gen Z wants *discovery*.

## Slide 3: The Solution (The What)
**"ShopDer: Making Green Addictive."**
*   **Discovery Commerce:**
    *   **Swipe Right:** To "Like" and add to cart (Instant gratification).
    *   **Swipe Left:** To ignore (Curated feed).
    *   **Swipe Up:** To see details/video.
*   **Green Gamification:**
    *   **Carbon Score:** Every item displays "Carbon Saved" (e.g., 5kg CO2).
    *   **Leaderboard:** Users compete for "Green Points" to become top savers.

## Slide 4: Product Demo (Visuals)
*   **Key Screens:**
    *   **Feed:** Full-screen vertical video/photo (TikTok style).
    *   **Profile:** Badges showing total CO2 saved.
    *   **Checkout:** Seamless escrow payment.

## Slide 5: Unique Value Proposition (UVP)
**"Why We Win where Others Fail."**
1.  **Fun (Discovery):** We replace "Search" with "Swipe."
2.  **Trust (Safety):** We hold payments until delivery (Escrow), solving the #1 scam fear.
3.  **Purpose (Green Living Lab):** We turn every purchase into a vote for the planet.

## Slide 6: Unfair Advantage (Market Data)
**"Riding the Wave of Gen Z Sustainability."**
*   **Market Size:** Thailand's Second-hand market is projected to reach **$18 Billion by 2031** [1].
*   **Growth Rate:** **22.8% CAGR** [1].
*   **Gen Z Power:** Controls **2.6 Trillion Baht** in purchasing power. 71% prefer buying online.
*   **Behavior:** 67% shop on impulse (Discovery Commerce) [3].

## Slide 7: Target User (The Who)
**"The Eco-Conscious Trendsetter"**
*   **Demographics:** Gen Z & Millennials (18-35), Urban, Digital Native.
*   **Psychographics:**
    *   Wants to be sustainable but won't sacrifice style or convenience.
    *   Loves the "thrill of the hunt" but hates the *effort* of thrifting.
    *   Socially conscious: Cares about their "Carbon Footprint."

## Slide 8: Market Positioning (The How)
**"High Discovery + High Trust"**
*   **The Matrix:**
    *   **X-Axis:** Search (Kaidee) vs. **Discovery (ShopDer)**.
    *   **Y-Axis:** Wild West (FB Marketplace) vs. **High Trust (ShopDer)**.
*   **Winning Zone:** ShopDer sits alone in the top-right quadrant (Discovery + Trust).

## Slide 9: Business Model (The Money)
**"Sustainable Revenue for a Sustainable Mission."**
*(See detailed strategy in `BUSINESS_MODEL_STRATEGY.md`)*

We use a **Tiered Membership Model** (Seed, Sprout, Forest) to align incentives with seller growth:
1.  **Seed (Free):** Entry level, 5% commission.
2.  **Sprout (Pro):** Monthly fee for reduced commission (3%) and 2x visibility.
3.  **Forest (Power):** Professional tier for 0% commission, 5x visibility, and advanced analytics.

**Green Data (Future):** Selling aggregated consumer preference data to brands to help them design better products.

## Slide 10: Channels (Go-to-Market)
**"Meeting Gen Z Where They Are."**
1.  **Social Ads:** TikTok & Instagram Reels (Video-first content).
2.  **Campus Ambassadors:** "Green Leaders" in universities to drive initial adoption.
3.  **Influencer Partnerships:** Fashion haulers doing "Thrift Flips" on ShopDer.

*   **Needs:** [Funding Amount] for Tech Development & Initial Marketing.
*   **Goal:** Reach 10,000 Active Users in Year 1.
